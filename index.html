TEMPERATURE ANOMALIES

OVER THE YEARS

In order to observe the impact of global warming, we measure the global
temperature anomaly through the years. A positive anomaly indicates that
the observed temperature was warmer than the average global temperature,
while a negative anomaly indicates that the observed temperature was cooler
than the average. From this graph, we can see that the anomalies are on
a steady increase which implies that the rate of global warming has increased.
